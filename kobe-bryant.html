Timeline of Kobe Bryant's Life

  • 1978 - Born in Philadelphia, PA
  • 1984 - Moved to Italy with his family
  • 1992 - Moved back to the USA
  • 1996 - Selected in the 1st round of the NBA Draft
  • 2000-2002 - Won 3 straight NBA Championships
  • 2009-2010 - Won his final 2 NBA Championships
  • 2016 - Retired from the NBA, scoring 60 points in his last game
  • 2020 - Passes away in a helicopter crash with his daughter Gigi and 7 other passengers.
